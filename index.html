About the studio

Elegant, controlled, and personal.

The Ink Lady is built for clients who want more than a quick tattoo. The focus is on clean design, careful consultation, and artwork that feels intentional on the body.

The studio aesthetic is dark, polished, and feminine without feeling limited to one audience. Minimal, symbolic, bold, delicate, or fully custom work can all begin with a clear conversation.
