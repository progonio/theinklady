Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Ink Lady handles personal information submitted through this website, WhatsApp, Instagram, and other contact channels used for tattoo and piercing appointments.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Who we are

The Ink Lady is a tattoo and piercing studio located in Prishtinë, Kosovo. For privacy questions, appointment changes, or requests about your personal information, contact us through WhatsApp or Instagram.

Studio location: The INK Lady, Ilir Konushevci 141, Prishtinë 10000, Kosovo.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to send when requesting an appointment or contacting the studio. This may include your name, phone number, tattoo style, preferred placement, preferred date, tattoo idea, reference details, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

If you contact us through WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps, or another third-party service, that service may also process your information under its own privacy policy.

3. How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to messages, discuss tattoo or piercing ideas, prepare appointments, confirm availability, provide aftercare or appointment information, improve the website, and keep basic business records.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Photos, references, and portfolio use

If you send tattoo references, body placement photos, or healed tattoo photos, we use them only to understand the requested work and communicate with you about the appointment. We will not intentionally publish identifiable client images on the website or social media without permission.

5. Cookies and third-party services

This website is designed as a simple business website and does not require an account. It may use embedded or linked third-party services, including Google Maps, WhatsApp, Instagram, and Google Fonts. These services may collect technical information such as IP address, device information, browser data, and interaction data according to their own policies.

If analytics, advertising pixels, or additional tracking tools are added later, this policy should be updated before publishing those changes.

6. Data retention

We keep appointment and communication information only for as long as needed to handle bookings, provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, or meet legal and accounting obligations.

7. Sharing information

We may share information with service providers when needed to operate the website, communicate with clients, manage appointments, or comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal information or share it for unrelated third-party marketing.

8. Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided, subject to reasonable business, legal, or security requirements. You can also choose not to submit information through the booking form and contact the studio directly instead.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information. However, no website, messaging platform, or internet transmission is completely secure, so sensitive information should not be sent unless necessary for the appointment.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the website, services, contact methods, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
